Student Wellbeing UI/UX Platform

A comprehensive role-based wellbeing support interface designed to enhance student mental health support through intuitive design and accessible features. This platform demonstrates strong UI/UX principles and user-centered design thinking.

Key Features:

  • Role-Based Access: Customized dashboards for Students, Supervisors, and Tutors with specific functionalities for each user type
  • Student Tools: Mood check-in system and integrated meeting booking functionality
  • Supervisor Dashboard: Real-time tracking and monitoring of student wellbeing updates
  • Tutor Overview: Comprehensive dashboard for monitoring multiple students and accessing analytics
  • Live Chat: Real-time communication simulation for immediate support
  • Emergency Support: Quick-access tools for crisis situations
  • Accessibility First: Responsive design ensuring access across all devices with WCAG compliance
